#### Core Platform Capabilities
- Data Ingestion
- Transformation
- Data Quality
- Governance & Observability
- API services

- **Ingestion:** Strategy for ingesting 22+ ERPs

- **Governance:** Define the "Single Pane of Glass" for the CISO (Craig). How do we tag PHI/Nuclear data?

**Deliverable:** Architecture Diagram & Governance strategy: Visualizing the "Hub" digesting the 22 ERPs

---

### Hour 3: Hub & Spoke Items + ACE Framework Execution

#### Hub & Spoke Items
- **Spokes:** What are the various lines of business and systems that will consume from the hub?
- **Spoke Enablement:** How do we use Delta Sharing to give Business Units their own "Spokes" without creating silos?
- Strategy to allow BUs to query Hub data without duplicating it

#### ACE Framework Items (The Execution)
- **Phase 2 (Cloud Connection):** Replacing daily batch uploads with real-time streams (Kafka/Spark Structured Streaming)
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Moving from Reactive (Break/Fix) to Proactive (Dynamic Alerting)

**Deliverable:** Data Flow Map: A visual showing data moving from Edge to Hub to Spoke

---

### Hour 4: Monetization & Advanced Capabilities

#### Hub & Spoke Items
- **Monetization (Spoke as a Product):** Turning internal data views into external products for customers
- **GenAI Integration:** Using Mosaic AI to query technical manuals (RAG) within the Hub to solve the Smart Technician use case

#### ACE Framework Items
- **Phase 3 (Airgap):** Validating the Databricks architecture for GovCloud / FedRAMP High
- **Phase 4 (Customer Clean Rooms):** The Westinghouse Strategy - sharing data securely cloud-to-cloud via Delta Sharing (The SaaS revenue model)

**Deliverable:** The SaaS Product Concept: A visual concept of what the customer buys in Year 4
